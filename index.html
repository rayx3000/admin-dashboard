Projects

Snake Game

Snake is a classic arcade-style game where you control a line (the “snake”) that moves around a grid, eating food to grow longer while avoiding running into walls or its own body. Each time the snake eats, it gets longer and the game speeds up, making it trickier to survive. The goal is simple: stay alive and get the highest score possible by eating as much as you can without crashing.

Calculator

A calculator is an electronic or software device used to perform mathematical operations like addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division. More advanced ones can handle complex functions such as trigonometry, logarithms, and equations. It’s basically a tool that speeds up and simplifies math by instantly giving accurate results.

Digital Clock

A digital clock is a device that displays the time using numbers instead of hands on a dial. It shows hours, minutes, and sometimes seconds on an electronic screen, often in a 12-hour or 24-hour format. It uses digital circuits to keep accurate time and is commonly found in phones, computers, and alarm clocks.

Search Bar

A search bar or search engine is a tool that lets users type in words or phrases to find information on the internet or within a specific app or website. The search engine processes the query, scans its database, and quickly shows the most relevant results—like web pages, images, or videos—based on what you asked for.

Crypto App

A crypto app is a mobile or desktop application that lets users buy, sell, store, and track cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin or Ethereum. It often includes features like live price charts, digital wallets, and security tools to manage crypto assets safely. Basically, it’s your digital hub for handling and monitoring everything related to cryptocurrency.

Sign Up Form

A sign-up form is an online form where users enter personal details—like their name, email, and password—to create an account on a website or app. It’s the first step to gaining access to personalized features, services, or content
